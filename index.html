About Me

I began drawing when I was about 11 years old. My older brother would always draw graffiti and I would watch him for hours. Creating a piece of art from a blank piece of paper, using nothing but your imagination and a pencil, was extremely rewarding. After years of just drawing graffiti, I wanted to graduate to something more challanging. I tried dozens of portraits but I quit on them before I got anywhere, because shading was so difficult. In 2017, I finished my first portrait and even though its on of my worst one's, it's probably the one I'm most proud of. It gave me the desire to become better and build on it, and most importantly I didnt give up on it like I had all the others previously.
